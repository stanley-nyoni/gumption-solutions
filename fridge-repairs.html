Fridge Not Cooling

A fridge that is not cold enough puts your food at risk and wastes electricity. The most common causes are a refrigerant gas leak, a blocked or dirty condenser coil, a faulty thermostat, or a compressor that is struggling. Our technicians carry diagnostic equipment to find the exact cause quickly and carry common spare parts so most cooling problems are fixed the same day.

Fridge Regassing in Cape Town

If your fridge is running but not getting cold, a gas leak is often to blame. We are certified to handle refrigerant gas safely. We first locate and repair the leak, then regas the system to the correct pressure. Attempting to regas without fixing the leak is a waste of money and we will always advise you honestly before proceeding.

Compressor Repairs

The compressor is the heart of your fridge. When it fails, the entire unit stops cooling. We diagnose and replace faulty compressors across all major brands. In some cases, a compressor replacement on a newer fridge is still far more cost-effective than buying a new appliance, and we will always give you an honest breakdown before any work begins.

Freezer Frost Build-Up and Defrost Problems

Excessive frost in the freezer is usually caused by a faulty defrost heater, a broken defrost thermostat, or a failed defrost timer. Left unattended, this leads to complete loss of cooling. We repair all defrost system faults across Samsung, LG, Defy, Bosch, and other brands.

Commercial Fridge Repairs Cape Town

A commercial refrigerator breakdown can cost a business thousands in lost stock. We provide urgent commercial fridge repair services across Cape Town for restaurants, guesthouses, B&Bs, wine farms, and retail outlets. We work on reach-in coolers, display fridges, bar fridges, undercounter units, and walk-in cold rooms. Fast response, minimal downtime.

Fridge Water Leaks and Ice Maker Faults

Water pooling under your fridge is typically caused by a blocked or frozen drain tube, a cracked drain pan, or a faulty water valve on models with ice makers and water dispensers. We fix all leak sources and ice maker faults across domestic and American-style double-door fridges.
