Tumble dryer faults we repair across Cape Town

Whether your dryer is not heating in Durbanville or the drum has stopped turning in Constantia, our technicians are on their way

Tumble Dryer Not Heating

A dryer that tumbles but produces no heat is one of the most common faults we repair in Cape Town. The usual causes are a failed heating element, a blown thermal fuse that has triggered as a safety measure, a faulty thermostat, or a defective timer. We carry heating elements and thermal fuses for most major brands and complete most heating repairs on the first visit.

Drum Not Turning or Belt Broken

If the drum is completely still when the dryer is running, the drive belt has almost certainly broken. This is one of the more straightforward tumble dryer repairs and we carry replacement belts for all major brands. A broken belt left unattended means the heating element continues to run without airflow, which can cause overheating and more serious damage.

Dryer Taking Too Long to Dry

Slow drying is almost always caused by restricted airflow. On vented dryers, a blocked or kinked vent hose is the most common cause. A lint filter that has not been cleaned regularly also significantly reduces airflow efficiency. On condenser dryers, a full or blocked condenser unit is usually responsible. We diagnose and clear the restriction or replace failing components.

Noisy Tumble Dryers

Squeaking usually means the drum support rollers or the drum glides are worn. Grinding often points to failing drum bearings. Thumping can indicate objects caught in the drum or worn support rollers. Rattling often means something has slipped through a pocket and is bouncing around inside the drum. We identify and fix all noise faults across vented and condenser dryers.

Overheating and Thermal Cut-Out

A dryer that shuts off after a short period is usually triggering its thermal cut-out protection due to a blocked vent, a clogged lint filter, or a failing thermostat that is not regulating temperature correctly. Continuing to use an overheating dryer is a fire risk. We diagnose overheating faults quickly and resolve the root cause.

Condenser and Heat Pump Dryer Repairs

Condenser dryers collect moisture in a removable container rather than venting outside. When the condenser unit becomes blocked with lint, drying efficiency drops significantly. Heat pump dryers are more complex and energy-efficient. We repair both types across Bosch, Siemens, Samsung, LG, Miele, and other brands with the correct diagnostic tools.
