Hisense Fridge Repairs Cape Town

Hisense Refrigerator Repairs

Hisense refrigerators range from compact bar fridges and mid-size fridge-freezers to large side-by-side and French-door models. Hisense fridges use inverter compressors for energy efficiency, and when these or other components develop faults, Gumption Solutions can diagnose and repair them quickly.

We repair all Hisense fridge models, handling compressor faults, thermostat issues, gas leaks, defrost system failures, ice maker problems, and door seal replacements. We carry Hisense fridge parts and serve all Cape Town suburbs.

Common Hisense fridge problems we fix:

  • Hisense fridge not cooling , internal temperature rising and food spoiling faster than usual
  • Frost building up in the Hisense freezer compartment and not defrosting automatically
  • Water dripping from the Hisense fridge or pooling on the kitchen floor
  • Hisense fridge compressor not starting or failing to maintain effective cooling
  • Unusual clicking, humming, or rattling noises from the back of the Hisense fridge
  • Hisense thermostat fault , fridge temperature fluctuating and not stabilising
  • Refrigerant low , Hisense fridge not cooling despite compressor running; recharge needed
  • Hisense fridge door seal cracked, worn, or not sealing the compartment properly
  • Error or fault codes displaying on the Hisense fridge digital panel

Hisense Washing Machine Repairs
