LG Fridge Repairs Cape Town

LG Refrigerator Repairs

LG refrigerators feature the award-winning Linear Compressor, Door-in-Door technology, and InstaView panels , all of which require specialist knowledge when something goes wrong. Whether your LG fridge is not cooling, the compressor is making noise, or the ice maker has stopped working, Gumption Solutions has the expertise to fix it.

We repair all LG fridge models sold in South Africa, including LG double-door, multi-door, French-door, and bottom-freezer models. We handle LG fridge error codes, compressor replacements, gas regassing, defrost system faults, and ice maker repairs across all Cape Town suburbs.

Common LG fridge problems we fix:

  • LG fridge not cooling properly , food spoiling faster than usual inside the refrigerator
  • Loud clicking, buzzing, or rattling sound coming from the LG compressor area
  • LG ice maker not working or producing hollow, misshapen, or no ice cubes
  • Frost building up in the LG freezer compartment despite the auto-defrost system
  • Water dripping from the LG dispenser or pooling on the floor beneath the fridge
  • LG Linear Compressor making abnormal noise or failing to maintain the correct temperature
  • LG InstaView panel not lighting up, responding to knocks, or displaying correctly
  • LG fridge error codes displayed (Er FF, Er dH, Er rF, Er 1F) requiring diagnosis
  • Refrigerant leak , LG fridge requires R600a gas recharge to restore proper cooling

LG Washing Machine Repairs Cape Town

LG Washing Machine Repairs

LG washing machines are renowned for their Inverter Direct Drive motors, 6 Motion technology, and TurboWash systems. LG front-loaders and LG top-loaders are popular across Cape Town homes, and when they develop faults, the Inverter Direct Drive system requires specific diagnostic expertise to repair correctly.

Gumption Solutions technicians are experienced with LG washing machine error codes, drum bearing replacements, pump faults, door seal repairs, and motor issues. We carry LG washing machine spare parts on every vehicle to ensure fast, first-visit repairs.

Common LG washing machine problems we fix:

  • LG washing machine drum barely spinning or stopping mid-cycle unexpectedly
  • LG washer not draining at end of cycle , OE error code displayed on the panel
  • LG washing machine door locked and refusing to release , dE error code showing
  • Water leaking from the LG door seal, drain hose, or base of the machine
  • Extremely loud during the LG spin cycle , drum bearings likely worn and need replacing
  • LG washing machine not filling with water , IE error code displayed on the panel
  • LG Inverter Direct Drive motor fault , machine not starting or completing cycles
  • Unbalanced load error (UE) continuously stopping the LG cycle from completing
  • LG LE error , washing machine motor overloaded, locked, or not responding correctly

LG Dishwasher Repairs

LG dishwashers include freestanding and built-in models with QuadWash and TrueSteam technology. When your LG dishwasher is not cleaning properly, displaying an error code, or leaking, Gumption Solutions technicians have the diagnostic tools and LG expertise to identify and fix the fault fast.

We repair LG dishwasher wash motors, pumps, heating elements, spray arms, door seals, and control boards. We work on all LG dishwasher models available in South Africa and carry the most commonly needed LG dishwasher spare parts on our vehicles.

Common LG dishwasher problems we fix:

  • LG dishwasher draining slowly or not draining at all , OE error code displayed

LG Tumble Dryer Repairs Cape Town

LG Tumble Dryer Repairs

LG tumble dryers include vented, condenser, and heat-pump models with Sensor Dry technology, Inverter motors, and ColdWash compatibility. When your LG dryer stops heating, the drum won't turn, or it's displaying a fault code, Gumption Solutions can diagnose and repair it quickly.

We repair LG tumble dryer heating elements, thermostats, drum belts, bearings, motors, condenser units, and PCB boards. We service LG vented dryers, LG condenser dryers, and LG heat-pump tumble dryers across all Cape Town suburbs.

Common LG tumble dryer problems we fix:

  • LG tumble dryer producing no heat , drum turns but clothes remain wet throughout
  • LG dryer drum completely still when switched on , motor or belt fault suspected
  • Clothes still damp or wet at the end of a complete LG drying cycle
  • Squeaking, squealing, or grinding noise coming from the LG dryer during operation
  • LG dryer fault codes displayed (D80, D90, D95, tE, HE) stopping the programme
  • LG condenser dryer not pumping water out into the collection tray correctly
  • LG heat-pump dryer running but taking significantly longer than normal to dry
  • LG tumble dryer tripping the household circuit breaker repeatedly on start-up
