Samsung Fridge Repairs Cape Town

Samsung Refrigerator Repairs

From Samsung twin-door fridges and side-by-side models to Samsung bottom-freezer and French-door refrigerators, we repair the full Samsung fridge range. Samsung fridges use advanced inverter compressors and Twin Cooling Plus technology, and when these systems develop faults, you need a technician who knows the Samsung diagnostic system.

Our technicians handle all Samsung fridge error codes, compressor replacements, gas regassing, defrost heater replacements, thermostat faults, and ice maker repairs. We also repair Samsung bar fridges and Samsung wine coolers across Cape Town.

Common Samsung Fridge Problems We Repair:

  • Samsung fridge not cooling properly or failing to maintain the correct temperature
  • Excessive frost or ice build-up in the freezer compartment
  • Water leaking from the refrigerator onto the floor
  • Samsung ice maker not producing ice or dispensing correctly
  • Compressor running continuously while the fridge remains warm
  • Twin Cooling Plus system not maintaining consistent temperatures
  • Samsung refrigerator error codes including 5E, 8E, 14E, 22E, and 40E
  • Damaged, loose, or worn door seals causing cold air loss
  • Refrigerant leak detection and regassing with R600a or R134a
