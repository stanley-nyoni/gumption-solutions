Bosch Fridge Repairs Cape Town

Bosch Refrigerator Repairs

Bosch refrigerators are engineered with VitaFresh technology to keep food fresher for longer, and SuperFreezing for rapid freezing. When a Bosch fridge is not cooling, the VitaFresh zones are not maintaining temperature, or the fridge is making unusual noises, Gumption Solutions can diagnose and repair it.

We repair all Bosch fridge models available in South Africa, including Bosch freestanding, built-in, and integrated refrigerators. We handle compressor faults, gas regassing, defrost heater replacements, thermostat faults, and electronic control board issues.

Common Bosch fridge problems we fix:

  • Bosch fridge not keeping food cold , internal temperature higher than the set point
  • Excessive frost forming in the Bosch freezer compartment despite NoFrost technology
  • Water pooling underneath the Bosch fridge or collecting inside the unit
  • Bosch VitaFresh drawer not maintaining the correct humidity or temperature zone
  • Bosch compressor not starting or cycling on and off rapidly without effective cooling
  • Unusual clicking or humming sounds coming from the back of the Bosch fridge
  • Bosch fridge door seal worn, cracked, or allowing warm air into the refrigerator
  • Error or fault codes showing on the Bosch digital fridge display panel
  • Refrigerant leak , Bosch fridge not cooling despite compressor running; gas recharge needed

Bosch Washing Machine Repairs Cape Town

Bosch Washing Machine Repairs

Bosch washing machines , including Bosch Serie 4, Serie 6, and Serie 8 models , are equipped with EcoSilence Drive motors, ActiveWater technology, and i-DOS automatic dosing. These advanced systems require specialist knowledge when they develop faults.

Our technicians carry Bosch washing machine spare parts on every callout and are experienced with Bosch error codes, drum bearing replacements, pump repairs, door seal replacements, and EcoSilence Drive motor faults. We repair both front-loader and top-loader Bosch washing machines.

Common Bosch washing machine problems we fix:

  • Bosch washing machine not spinning , cycle stopping before the spin begins
  • Bosch washer not draining at end of cycle , F21 or E21 error code displayed
  • Bosch washing machine door lock fault , machine refusing to start (F34 error)
  • Water leaking from the Bosch door seal, detergent drawer, or base of the machine
  • Extremely noisy during the Bosch spin cycle , drum bearings suspected worn
  • Bosch EcoSilence Drive motor fault , error code displayed or motor not running
  • Error or fault codes (F01–F43, E17, E18, E23) stopping the Bosch wash programme
  • Bosch i-DOS automatic dosing system not dispensing detergent or softener correctly
  • Bosch washing machine not responding to any button presses or control inputs

Bosch Dishwasher Repairs Cape Town

Bosch Dishwasher Repairs

Bosch dishwashers are among the finest available in South Africa, from the quiet Bosch Serie 4 to the ultra-quiet Serie 8 models. Bosch dishwashers feature PerfectDry, SpeedPerfect, and EcoSilence Drive technology. When a Bosch dishwasher develops a fault, Gumption Solutions has the diagnostic expertise to fix it right.

We repair Bosch dishwasher pumps, heating elements, spray arms, door seals, hinge assemblies, and control boards. We service all Bosch dishwasher models including freestanding, built-in, and integrated units across Cape Town.

Common Bosch dishwasher problems we fix:

  • Bosch dishwasher not cleaning dishes properly , food residue or cloudy film left on items
  • Standing water in the bottom of the Bosch dishwasher after the cycle ends (E25 error)
  • Bosch dishwasher leaking from the door , door seal perished or damaged
  • Bosch PerfectDry not working , dishes remaining wet at the end of the programme
  • Error codes (E09, E15, E23, E24, E25) displayed on the Bosch dishwasher panel
  • Bosch dishwasher not starting or completely unresponsive , no power to the machine
  • Bosch dishwasher door spring or hinge broken , door will not stay open safely
  • Loud grinding or rattling noise during the Bosch dishwasher wash cycle

Bosch Tumble Dryer Repairs Cape Town

Bosch Tumble Dryer Repairs

Bosch tumble dryers include vented, condenser, and heat-pump models (Serie 4, 6, and 8) with SelfCleaning Condenser, AutoDry sensor technology, and EcoSilence Drive motors. Bosch dryers are built to last, but when they develop faults, specialist knowledge is needed.

We repair Bosch tumble dryer heating elements, thermostats, drum belts, bearings, condenser units, heat exchangers, and PCB boards. We service all Bosch dryer types across Cape Town, from vented models to heat-pump units.

Common Bosch tumble dryer problems we fix:

  • Bosch tumble dryer drum turning but producing no heat at all
  • Bosch dryer drive belt snapped , drum completely static and not rotating
  • Clothes still damp or wet after a full 90-minute Bosch drying cycle
  • Squeaking or squealing loudly , Bosch drum bearings or idler pulley worn
  • Bosch SelfCleaning Condenser blocked , poor drying performance and longer cycle times
  • Error codes (E00–E50) interrupting or stopping the Bosch drying programme
  • Bosch heat-pump tumble dryer running but not reaching the correct drying temperature
  • Bosch tumble dryer tripping the household circuit breaker repeatedly on start-up
