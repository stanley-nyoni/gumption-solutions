What causes washing machine problems?

Understanding the fault helps you decide whether to call us right away or wait. Here is what our Cape Town technicians find most often.

Washing Machine Not Spinning

A machine that stops mid-cycle or refuses to spin is one of the most common calls we receive across Cape Town. The fault is usually a worn drive belt, a failing motor, a blocked drain pump that prevents the spin safety system from engaging, or an overloaded drum causing the machine to stop for balance protection. We diagnose the exact cause and carry the most common belts, pumps, and motor components.

Washing Machine Leaking Water

Leaking washing machines in Cape Town homes are almost always caused by a damaged door seal on front loaders, a cracked pump housing, worn drum bearings that allow water to escape at the shaft, or loose inlet hose connections. A leak left unattended can cause significant water damage to flooring and cabinetry. We respond quickly and fix leaks of all types across all brands.

Error Codes and Electronic Faults

Modern washing machines display error codes when something goes wrong. Samsung E1, LG OE, Bosch F21, and Defy error codes all point to specific faults. Our technicians are trained to read and diagnose error codes across all major brands and reset or repair the underlying fault rather than simply clearing the display.

Front Loader vs Top Loader Repairs

We repair both front loaders and top loaders across all brands. Front loaders are popular in Cape Town homes for their water efficiency but are prone to door seal deterioration, drum bearing wear, and drainage issues. Top loaders are simpler mechanically but still experience pump failures, lid switch problems, and agitator faults. We handle both with equal expertise.

Noisy Washing Machines

A washing machine that bangs, grinds, squeaks, or rattles during a cycle usually has worn drum bearings, foreign objects caught between the drum and the outer tub, or worn suspension rods. Noise problems tend to get worse over time if left untreated. We diagnose noise faults across Cape Town and replace worn bearings and drum components same-day where parts are available.

Washing Machine Not Heating Water

If your clothes are coming out dirty or the wash seems cold, the heating element may have failed. This is a common fault across many brands and is usually a straightforward repair. We test the element, thermostat, and temperature sensor to identify the exact component that needs replacing.
